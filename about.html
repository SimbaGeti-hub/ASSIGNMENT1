When We Started!



Simba Activewear was founded in 2023 with a bold vision to create workout gear that goes beyond performance. We wanted to design activewear that celebrates strength, culture, and confidence—especially for women who want to express their identity through every move they make.

Why We Started!



Simba Activewear was born from a passion to create more than just workout clothes. We saw a need for activewear that not only performs but also reflects the strength, culture, and identity of women everywhere. Our goal was to build a brand that empowers women to move confidently and boldly while honoring their unique stories and heritage.

How We Work!



Our process centers on listening to women’s real needs. We use high-quality, breathable fabrics that stand up to any workout, paired with ethical manufacturing practices. Each collection is carefully designed, tested, and refined to ensure comfort, style, and durability.

What We Prioritize



At Simba, we prioritize comfort, confidence, and inclusivity. Our designs flatter all body types and empower women to feel strong and proud. We also focus on sustainability, ethical production, and uplifting a community of bold, fearless women.

You Can Trust Us



Trust is the foundation of Simba Activewear. From sourcing to delivery, we maintain transparency and commitment to quality. When you choose Simba, you’re choosing a brand that respects your journey and delivers gear you can rely on every step of the way.

OUR MISSION
